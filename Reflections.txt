Throughout the development of the Pet Help Center Case Management System, we gained a deeper understanding of how to plan, architect, and deliver a fully functional full-stack application from a real-world specification. This project challenged us to read complex requirements, break them down into actionable tasks, and validate each feature carefully. We strengthened our skills with React, Express, SQLite, and modern development workflows while also learning how to design clean data models, protect routes securely, and build robust ingestion and reporting pipelines. Implementing features such as case normalization, pre-upload editing, soft-delete/restore, admin management, and reporting gave us practical experience with full-stack integration and debugging across both the frontend and backend layers.
